My Story

The Heart of TJR Mind & Body

My Scoliosis Journey

My journey began at 12 with scoliosis, a condition necessitating multiple spinal surgeries. In 2016 alone, I underwent four spinal surgeries for issues ranging from aborted initial attempts to infections and implant stabilisation. In 2022, broken implants required further surgery and a prolonged recovery.

Most recently, I faced potential surgery again, with unbearable, constant pain severely limiting my mobility even with medication, as specialists investigated the suspected impact on the vertebra below my spinal fusion.

Finding My Path to Healing

Eventually, self-help became crucial alongside medical treatment. The increased openness around mental health encouraged me to explore alternative therapies. Chinese medicine and yoga offered some relief, and I've incorporated meditation and journaling — practices I consistently return to because I recognise their benefit.

Despite this, addressing all dimensions of well-being — physical, mental, emotional, social, and spiritual — remains an ongoing, evolving journey I navigate with resilience and curiosity.

My Approach to Holistic Wellness Coaching

This path ultimately led me to become a holistic wellness coach. Following redundancy from a corporate career, I felt a strong calling to support others facing similar health and life challenges, recognising the gaps in traditional support systems.

I bring lived experience and genuine empathy to my coaching practice, helping clients build resilience, navigate chronic pain, and create meaningful change — one step at a time.
