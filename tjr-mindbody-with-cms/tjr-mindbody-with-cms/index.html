My Authentic Story

From Chronic Pain to Purpose

My journey began at 12 with scoliosis — a condition that led to multiple spinal surgeries throughout my life. Through that experience I developed a deep understanding of chronic pain, resilience, and the power of holistic self-care.

That lived experience is now the foundation of my coaching practice. I'm not just a coach who read the textbook — I've lived the challenges you're facing, and I'm here to walk alongside you.
