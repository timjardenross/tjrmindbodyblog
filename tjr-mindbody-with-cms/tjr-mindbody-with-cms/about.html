My Story

The Heart of TJR Mind & Body

X-ray of Tim's scoliosis — the journey that started it all

📍 Melbourne, Australia
1 The Beginning

My Scoliosis Journey

My journey began at 12 with scoliosis, a condition necessitating multiple spinal surgeries throughout my life. In 2016 alone, I underwent four spinal surgeries for issues ranging from aborted initial attempts to infections and implant stabilisation.

In 2022, broken implants required further surgery and a prolonged recovery. Most recently, I faced potential surgery again — with unbearable, constant pain severely limiting my mobility even with medication, as specialists investigated the suspected impact on the vertebra below my spinal fusion.

"Living with this level of pain for so long forces you to find a different relationship with your body — one built on patience, not resistance."

2 The Turning Point

Finding My Path to Healing

Eventually, self-help became crucial alongside medical treatment. The increased openness around mental health encouraged me to explore alternative therapies. Chinese medicine and yoga offered some relief, and I've incorporated meditation and journaling — practices I consistently return to because I recognise their benefit.

Despite this, addressing all dimensions of well-being — physical, mental, emotional, social, and spiritual — remains an ongoing, evolving journey I navigate daily with resilience and curiosity. That complexity is something I now embrace rather than fight.

3 Where I Am Now

My Approach to Holistic Wellness Coaching

This path ultimately led me to become a holistic wellness coach. Following redundancy from a corporate career, I felt a strong calling to support others facing similar health and life challenges — recognising the gaps in traditional support systems that I had experienced firsthand.

I bring lived experience and genuine empathy to my coaching practice, helping clients build resilience, navigate chronic pain, and create meaningful change. I'm not here to tell you what worked for me and expect it to work for you — I'm here to help you find what works for you.

"I understand what it's like when the system falls short. My job is to help you fill those gaps with something that actually fits your life."
