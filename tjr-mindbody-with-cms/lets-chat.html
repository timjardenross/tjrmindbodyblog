Let's See if We're a Good Fit

This free 30-minute call is a relaxed, no-pressure conversation to understand what you're going through and explore whether my coaching approach aligns with your wellness goals.

Think of it as a "getting to know you" chat — there's no commitment, no sales pitch, just an honest conversation about where you are and where you want to be.

  • ✓ What we'll cover: Your current wellness challenges and what's been holding you back
  • ✓ Your goals: What lasting well-being looks like for you specifically
  • ✓ How I work: My approach, what coaching looks like in practice, and how we'd work together
  • ✓ No obligation: At the end you decide whether it feels right — completely your call
